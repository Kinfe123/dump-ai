## Why Dumpai?
- Integration of AI with web analytics tools is crucial for understanding user behavior and optimizing website performance.
- The combination of AI and web analytics enables businesses to extract valuable insights and make data-driven decisions.
- The project aims to seamlessly integrate AI technologies with existing web analytics tools.
- The integrated system tracks website metrics and facilitates real-time, interactive chat-based discussions.
- The chat-based discussions capture and analyze crucial information about the integrated website.
- This innovative approach empowers businesses to make informed decisions and unlock the full potential of their digital presence.
